Our Story

Building a Legacy
of Empowerment

Founded in 2014, AfriImpact began as a small group of innovators with a shared vision: to see a thriving, self-sufficient Africa powered by its own people.

Our Mission

To catalyze sustainable development across Africa by empowering communities through innovative education, climate resilience, and youth-led social entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

An Africa where every individual has the resources and skills to drive progress, fostering a continent of equity, prosperity, and environmental harmony.

Integrity

Transparency in every action and dollar spent.

Inclusion

Empowering the marginalized and unheard voices.

Sustainability

Long-term solutions for lasting impact.

Collaboration

Working together with global and local partners.
